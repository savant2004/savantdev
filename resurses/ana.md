# SAVANT — SCROLLTELLING EXPERIENCE

## Philosophy

The website is not a portfolio.

The website is a journey.

Every animation must tell part of the story.

The story is:

Chaos
→ Intelligence
→ Systems
→ Products
→ Impact

The visitor should feel like they are entering a living digital ecosystem rather than browsing a website.

# GLOBAL MOTION SYSTEM

## Camera

The entire website behaves like a camera moving through a digital universe.

Never use abrupt transitions.

Everything should feel connected.

Movement style:

* Smooth
* Cinematic
* Organic
* Purposeful

Libraries:

* GSAP ScrollTrigger
* Framer Motion
* Lenis
* SVG Path Animations

# PHASE 1 — LOADING EXPERIENCE

Duration:

2–3 seconds

---

## Scene 1

Screen is completely black.

No logo.

No text.

Only a few tiny purple pixels.

Randomly scattered.

They gently flicker.

---

## Scene 2

More pixels begin appearing.

Thousands of pixels.

They slowly start moving.

The movement resembles a wave.

Not random.

Like a swarm.

Like intelligence forming.

---

## Scene 3

Pixels begin following invisible SVG paths.

The paths are not visible.

The user only sees the movement.

The wave travels horizontally.

---

## Scene 4

The wave suddenly bends.

Pixels begin assembling.

The SAVANT logo slowly appears.

Each pixel locks into position.

The logo is formed entirely from pixels.

---

## Scene 5

Background remains alive.

Thousands of transparent pixels continue floating behind the logo.

The logo feels stable.

The environment feels alive.

